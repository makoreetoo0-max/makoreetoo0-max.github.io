About

Who I am

I am a Computer Science student with a strong and growing interest in cybersecurity, software development, and systems programming. I am currently exploring various specializations within the field, which I believe is a valuable and necessary stage in my academic journey.

My learning approach is predominantly hands-on. I find that I learn most effectively through building projects, solving complex problems, and occasionally writing code on paper to better visualize and understand system behavior. I firmly believe that developing a solid foundation in core principles is more important than committing to a narrow specialization too early.

Outside of my studies, I actively immerse myself in the field of cybersecurity. I spend considerable time studying real-world system vulnerabilities, analyzing exploitation techniques, and exploring defensive strategies to mitigate them.
